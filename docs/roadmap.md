# ForgeFit Roadmap

## Vision

ForgeFit is a fitness companion built to help users track their workouts, monitor their progress, and preserve their fitness journey over time.

---

## Version 1.0 (Core Features)

* User Authentication
* Dashboard
* Workout Logging
* Workout History
* Profile Management
* Weight Tracking

---

## Version 1.5

* Personal Record (PR) Tracking
* Progress Charts
* Rest Timer
* Exercise Notes
* Plate Calculator

---

## Version 2.0

* Progress Photos
* Gym Memories
* Workout Streaks
* Achievements and Badges
* PDF Export

---

## Version 3.0

* AI Workout Suggestions
* Recovery Score
* Form Analysis
* Smart Notifications

---

## Future Ideas

* Apple Watch Integration
* Wear OS Support
* Community Challenges
* Trainer Mode
* Multi-device Sync

---

## Personal Goal

To build a fitness application that I would genuinely use every day and continue improving throughout my fitness journey.
